Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Secure Vault. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your data when you visit our website.

2. Data Encryption & Storage

Secure Vault operates on a "Zero-Knowledge" architecture:

  • Client-Side Encryption: All messages are encrypted on your device using AES-256 before being sent to our database.
  • No Plain Text Storage: We (the administrators) cannot read your messages because we do not possess your password/secret key.
  • Data Storage: Encrypted strings are stored using SheetDB and Google Sheets APIs.

3. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. Log Files

Like many other Web sites, Secure Vault makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information.
